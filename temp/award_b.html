Who we are

A foundation that helps you flourish

Welcome to Shubham Group, your premier destination for luxury real estate in Navi Mumbai and Panvel. Established in 2010 by Mr. Gurunath and Mr. Dilip Patil, we specialize in crafting exquisite, affordable living spaces. Our commitment to superior construction quality earned us the Best Construction Quality Award from former Maharashtra Chief Minister, Mr. Devendra Fadnavis.

With 500+ homes delivered over 20,000 square meters, we prioritize customer satisfaction and foster strong relationships. Experience the Shubham Group difference—where luxury meets affordability, and your dream home awaits with comfort, elegance, and unmatched architectural excellence. Discover our diverse range of projects, each meticulously designed to cater to your lifestyle needs, ensuring a harmonious blend of functionality and aesthetic appeal.
