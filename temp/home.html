Who we are

Shubham Group: Transforming Houses into Homes

Welcome to Shubham Group, where luxury meets affordability. Founded in 2010 by Mr. Gurunath Balaram Patil and Mr. Dilip Balaram Patil, we redefine lifestyles with exquisite, cost-effective living spaces.

As a leading real estate builder and developer in Navi Mumbai and Panvel, we are committed to excellence.With over 500 homes delivered across 20,000 square meters, our superior construction quality has earned us the Best Construction Quality Award from former Maharashtra Chief Minister, Mr. Devendra Fadnavis. At Shubham Group, we make luxurious living affordable while fostering strong relationships with our customers. Discover our projects and experience unparalleled comfort and grandeur.
