Career

The success of the Group is because of the values that are dear to us as an organization. We believe that all our actions relating to our job in the Shubham group must also reflect these values.

  • Innovation - We aim to excel in all areas, including products, service, safety, value, human relations, competitiveness, and profitability.
  • Quality - We strive to lead in business growth, profitability, and technology by providing top-quality products and services that set the standard in their class.
  • Trust & Respect - Believing that people are our strength, we will promote teamwork and ensure that employees treat each other with trust and respect.
  • Customer Focus - We prioritize identifying customer needs and delivering products and services that effectively address them.
  • Action Orientation - Speed and agility are crucial competitive strengths that keep us ahead in today's rapidly changing world.
  • Responsibility to Society - We acknowledge our obligation to society by integrating high ethical and social standards into all our business practices.

How We Hire

If you are enthusiastic about a career with the Shubham Group, you'll want to know how and why we select individuals to join our team. Our hiring decisions are based on an impartial assessment of your skills, experience, and competencies.

The actual steps involved in recruitment follow a well defined path:

  • Shortlisting the application - In this step, we shortlist the received CVs from various sources based on their suitability for the open position.
  • Preliminary Assessment - Shortlisted candidates undergo an initial interview round focusing on functional competencies, as well as assessing aptitude and attitude.
  • Final Interview - Candidates who pass the initial interview proceed to a subsequent interview with one or more functional heads. During this stage, the final decision considers all tangible and intangible aspects of your personality.
