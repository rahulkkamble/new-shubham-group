Shubham Builders

Shubham Group was incorporated in the year 2010 by Mr. Gurunath Balaram Patil and Dilip Balaram Patil, is one of the established Builders and Developers Company, catering Key Area of Navi Mumbai and Panvel. Shubham Group have successfully handed over about 500+ Houses across Navi Mumbai of about 20,000 Square Meters area. The Company is awarded with Best Construction Quality handed over by Former Chief Minister of Maharashtra Mr. Devendra Fadnavis.

The company is one of the fastest growing entities in the real-estate sector with a difference that offers luxury homes for reasonable costs, with excellent customer care levels with highest customer satisfaction index and the highest imaginable standards for the welfare of its staff, society as well as for the environment. While corporate has gone from strength to strength, the Shubham Group is primarily synonymous with quality, commitment towards customers, reliability, and excellence in architecture.

Shubham Groups success comes from its ability to deliver on time, excellent customer service, maintaining high quality standards and most important its ability to introduce new concepts which remains unmatched till date.
