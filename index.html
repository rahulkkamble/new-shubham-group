Who we are

Shubham Group: Redefining Navi Mumbai's Real Estate Landscape

Shubham Group is established with the sole purpose of creating spaces that elevate lifestyles and showcase the finest in luxury living.

Shubham Group was incorporated in the year 2010 by Mr. Gurunath Balaram Patil and Dilip Balaram Patil, is one of the established Builders and Developers Company, catering Key Area of Navi Mumbai and Panvel. Shubham Group have successfully handed over about 500+ Houses across Navi Mumbai of about 20,000 Square Meters area. The Company is awarded with Best Construction Quality handed over by Former Chief Minister of Maharashtra Mr. Devendra Fadnavis.
